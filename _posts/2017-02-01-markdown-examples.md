## 🌅 Morning Routine
I start my day early in the morning.  
I usually wake up, freshen up, and have a cup of tea.  
This helps me feel energetic and ready for the day.

## 📚 Studying
After breakfast, I focus on my studies.  
I dedicate 2–3 hours to learning programming, web development, and other academic subjects.  
I take notes and practice coding exercises to improve my skills.

## 💻 Online Work
In the afternoon, I work on online projects and assignments.  
I also explore GitHub repositories and improve my personal blog.  
This helps me gain practical experience in technology and development.


## 📝 Evening Routine
In the evening, I review what I learned during the day.  
I plan tasks for the next day and ensure all work is completed.  
Sometimes, I relax by reading tech blogs or watching tutorials online.

## 🌙 Conclusion
Having a structured routine of studying and online work keeps me organized.  
It motivates me to learn continuously and build my skills in technology.
