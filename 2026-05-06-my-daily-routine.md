## 🌅 Morning

I wake up early every morning. I freshen up, offer prayers, and have tea with breakfast.

## 📚 Study Time

From 9 AM to 12 PM, I study programming and work on my coding projects.

## 🍽️ Afternoon

After lunch, I take a short rest and then continue learning or watching tutorials.

## 🏃 Evening

In the evening, I go for a walk or do some exercise to stay healthy.

## 🌙 Night

At night, I review what I learned during the day, read a little, and sleep by 11 PM.
